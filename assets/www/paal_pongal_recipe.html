Ingredients

  • 1 liter Milk
  • 1 tablespoon Uncooked rice
  • 4 tablespoons Sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom powder
  • Dried fruit for garnish
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ghee

Instructions

Wash the rice.

Add milk and rice to a thick bottom pan.

Cook over a slow flame till the quantity is reduced by half. Keep stirring to avoid burning.

Add sugar and continue cooking on a low flame until it thickens.

Add cardamom powder and garnish with dry fruits or nuts such as cashew nuts roasted in ghee.
